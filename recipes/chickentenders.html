Chicken Tenders

Description

Chicken tenders are delicious, I could probably eat them everyday.
This recipe is fairly easy and convenient allowing you to enjoy them in 20 minutes or less.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Costco Chicken Tenders

Steps

  1. Place frozen chicken tenders into air fryer of choice
  2. Set temperature to 300 Celcius for 20 minutes
  3. Flip the chicken tenders at 10 minutes for even cooking
  4. Once finished cooking, enjoy with or without sauce of choice
